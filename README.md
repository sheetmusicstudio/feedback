# Score Feedback & Support
> **Official repository for reporting sheet music errors and notation improvements.**

Thank you for purchasing our scores! To ensure the highest quality, we actively collect feedback from our community. If you find any discrepancies or have suggestions for better readability, please let us know.


## 🛠 How to Report
If you find a typo, a wrong note, or a notation issue, please follow these steps:

1. **Search:** Check the [Issues](https://github.com/sheetmusicstudio/feedback/issues) tab to see if it's already reported.
2. **New Issue:** Click the **'New Issue'** button and use the **'Score Correction'** template.
3. **Details:** Specify the **Piece Title**, **Measure Number**, and **Instrument**.
4. **Evidence:** Attach a screenshot or reference audio timestamp if possible.

## 🎁 Contributor Rewards
We value your time and expertise. When your contribution is verified and implemented:
- You will receive the **newly updated version** of the score.
- A **discount coupon** for your next purchase will be sent to your email.


## ⚠️ Guidelines
- **Personal Use Only:** Do not upload or share the full PDF file in public issues.
- **Account Security:** If unauthorized file sharing is detected, your account may be subject to suspension.
- **Support:** For payment or account-related issues, please contact us via email instead of opening an issue.
